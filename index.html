About Me

Currently I am a Project Manager II, with experience managing projects and also acting as a business analyst for consumer packaged goods, medical and pharmaceutical clients. Prior to my current posistion I held a dual role as an administrator for SAP and the sole resource for managing the SAP MM module, used for purchasing goods and services globally. I have also worked as a help desk analyst.

I have always loved technology and enjoyed jobs where that was my focus. Learning and improving upon existing skills has also been of paramount importance. I was able to combine both passions when I decided to learn full stack web developement.

My attention to detail, organizational skills, investment in learning and interpersonal skills have served me well in every position I have held. As an SAP administrator and MM module SME, I had unique insight into not only the system as a whole, but also end user processes. This allowed me to troubleshoot, streamline specific functions and provide training to all users.

As a current student learning full stack web development, I look forward to returning to my technology roots and transitioning into a new and rewarding career.
